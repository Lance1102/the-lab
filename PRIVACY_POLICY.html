Privacy Policy for OrbitFlow

Last Updated: February 05, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how OrbitFlow ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

TL;DR: We do not track your browsing history. Your dashboard data is stored locally on your device or in your own private Google Drive (if you enable sync). We do not have access to your personal data.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Local Data Storage

OrbitFlow stores your dashboard configuration (links, groups, layout settings, custom icons) locally on your device using the chrome.storage API. This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly enable the Sync feature.

Google Account Data (Optional Sync Feature)

If you choose to sign in with Google to use the "Sync & Backup" feature:

  • We use the identity permission to facilitate authentication.
  • We request access to a specialized, hidden folder in your Google Drive (Application Data folder, via https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata).
  • Your dashboard data is encrypted and backed up to this private folder.
  • We cannot see, access, or share your files in Google Drive. We can only access the specific configuration file created by OrbitFlow.

2. Permissions Explained

We request the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function:

  • Storage: Required to save your dashboard preferences and links effectively on your computer.
  • Identity: Required only for authenticating with Google to enable the cloud backup feature.

3. Third-Party Services

This extension interacts with Google APIs strictly for the purpose of authentication and data synchronization as described above. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties.

4. Data Retention

Since data is stored locally or in your personal Google Drive, you have full control over it. Uninstalling the extension will remove local data. You can delete the backup file from your Google Drive at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.
